About Me

Hi! My name is Carter Quesenberry. I graduated from Utah Valley University, with a bachelors is Geography, minor in Computer Science, and Certificate of Proficiency in Geographic Information Systems (GIS). I have worked on various GIS projects, and am excited to showcase them!

I'm a passionate about GIS and cartography, especially in a way that helps the public. I have expertise in spatial analysis, remote sensing, and geographic data visualization. My work focuses on creating meaningful maps and analyses that help organizations make informed decisions about their spatial challenges.

I love all things maps, I love to read and watch movies, and I find joy in the details. When I’m not mapping or coding, you can find me exploring local coffee shops, catching up on a good book, or enjoying a cozy movie night with friends. I’m excited to keep growing as a GIS professional and to contribute to projects that make a real difference.
